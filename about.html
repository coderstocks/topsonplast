    About Us

    One motto To bring safety home.

    A decade long journey with one motto – to bring safety home. For a decade we have dedicated our efforts in designing and providing the safest and cost effective solutions related to modern day electrical fittings , making them available for all while delivering optimum quality. Topson Plast aims to revolutionize the electrical industry with every landmark we achieve.

    Our Story

    Since 1995

    TopsonPlast was established in the year 1995 by the founding chairman Mr. Sanjay Sharma. His insights and understanding took the company to greater heights within a short span since he had an in-depth understanding of the requirements of the masses in general. Today TopsonPlast has become a brand name among the rural dwellers with more than 145 designs to offer and many more to join the family of the existing designs.

    Our distributor network spreads across all major cities and states in India. We foresee our wide range of products to be present in every state of India and dreams to take it globally by the year 2025. We are looking to acquire new distributors to make our dreams a reality.

    We are into detailed designing by our in-house team of professional designers and our designs are simple but unique which is the cultivation straight out from our thought and R&D process.

    Our Quality

    We value quality over everything else and we are dedicated towards ensuring it. Each of our products are passed through a process of carefully crafted quality assurance checks to ensure each product has optimum quality, which isn’t compromised by quantity while keeping the norms of security measures in mind.

    Our Mission and Vision

    Since decades we have strived to perfect quality while keeping in mind the demands of the population. Ensuring safety for all is our prime mission and making every home equipped with safer accessories at low cost is our vision. We want our future to grow and flourish in a secure environment.

    Our Community

    The man-force and technological assets that we have in our firm have made it simple for us to produce high quality products. As a customer-focused and reliable firm in the market, we have great goals to achieve and maintain a level of excellence in future too.
