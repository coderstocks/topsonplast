    Know More About Company

    #1 Manufacturer in Electrical Accessories

    Beginning with just an epiphany “Topson“ to becoming the most trusted brand in the industry of electrical fittings manufacturing while staying true to its name: TOPSON- Top quality. From being a small door to door distribution company to an established name in the industry, for 27 years facing all ups and downs we grew and expanded all over India. Starting from one factory and one machine to having 10+ factories with more than 30 injection molding machines we reached the heights but were always anchored to the ground by our values. With only one resolve in our mind building a safer tomorrow for all !!
